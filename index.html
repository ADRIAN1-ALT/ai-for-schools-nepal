🧠 What is AI?
Artificial Intelligence — or AI — is when computers are taught to think and learn like humans! 🤯

Just like how you learn to recognise a cat 🐱 by seeing many cats, AI learns by looking at thousands and thousands of examples.

AI is already all around you — in your phone's face unlock, in YouTube recommendations, and even in Nepali hospitals helping doctors! 🏥

Fun Fact: The word "Artificial" means "made by humans", and "Intelligence" means "the ability to learn and solve problems." Together = AI! 🚀
